Ingrid Derrickson - The Artist

Bio / CV

Contemporary American painter working in oil and acrylic. Figurative, Impressionistic, Plein Air.

Former Dancer at Ethno-American Dance Theatre.

Studied Interior Design/Architecture at the O'More School of Design.

Former School Administrator/instructor for Nashville Ballet.

Lives in Middle Tennessee on a small hobby farm, Justamere Farm. Enjoying the peace of the country, surrounding wildlife, my husabnd and grandchildren, and many beloved pets.
